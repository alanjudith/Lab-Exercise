CLASSICAL MUSIC!

Classical music is a genre of music that emerged in the late 17th century and reached its peak during the Classical period (approximately 1750-1820).
It is known for its complexity, structural intricacy, and emphasis on instrumental compositions.
Classical music is characterized by its use of formal structures, such as sonatas, symphonies, concertos, and operas.
These compositions often follow specific patterns and adhere to established rules of harmony and counterpoint. The music is typically written down in musical notation, allowing for precise interpretation and performance.
